Additional Reading and Review

Online Reading (optional):
If you need some reinforcement on the material covered in this module, I recommend two websites:
  • w3schools.com

This site is helpful when you want a quick tip or a light overview. (Warning: This site is a bit controversial, and is looked down on in some circles. But overall, I think they do a reasonable job of presenting the bare basics on most topics. It can be very helpful to beginners who want to get the gist of a subject without encountering too much advanced nomenclature.)

For instance, read the sections HTML Home through HTML Images.

  • MDN

Gain a deeper understanding of how HTML or other web-related technologies work. Most of what you read on MDN is definitive. 

Additional Info:
Visit and explore the following site which is a also definitive, but often harder to understand than either MDN or W3Schools:


The World Wide Web Consortium

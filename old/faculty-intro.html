About Ron Austin


My professional passions are Instructional Design, Programming, and Digital Media. I earned my BA at The Evergreen State College in 1991 where I studied communications and computer science. I received a Master of Education from the University of Washington in 2005.

I attended Bellevue College as a student in the late 1990s to earn a certificate in Web Authoring. After I graduated from BC I contracted with multiple places including Microsoft, GoMobile, Limelight Media, The Creative Group, William Meacham Insurance, and The Museum of Radio and Electricity. My work has varied between client-side web development and video editing. I am currently the E-Learning Specialist with the Healthcare Informatics BAS Program at Bellevue College. This gives me a lot of opportunity to use HTML and Javascript on a daily basis.

I’ve been an instructor since 2000. The subjects that I have taught vary quite a bit between web development, animation, and history of film for example.

On the personal side of things I enjoy getting outside hiking and running around.  I try to  participate in several 5k & 10ks a year. I bike and ski if the weather is not too rainy.  I also have a kid who turned 18 this year and rides a unicycle and skateboards a lot.
